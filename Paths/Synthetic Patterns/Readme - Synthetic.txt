Synthetic path data from: 

Performance of redirected walking algorithms in a constrained virtual world
E Hodgson, E Bachmann, T Thrash
IEEE transactions on visualization and computer graphics 20 (4), 579-587

https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/iel7/2945/6777423/06777456.pdf

These paths were used to compare different redirected walking algorithms in specific, idealized movement patterns (e.g., a long and perfectly straight path vs. a curved figure 8 path vs. a square figure 8 with straight segments and 90 degree turns, etc.)
